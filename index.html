We are a private company founded by Mr. Omar Ben Achour, operating exclusively in Algeria, specifically in the Biskra province. Established at the beginning of 2026 under its current name, the company has been actively involved in various local collaborations with national enterprises.

We specialize in the packaging and export of fresh fruits and vegetables, with a special focus on the renowned Algerian Deglet Nour dates. These exceptional dates are sourced from the vast Algerian desert, with Biskra province being the heart of their cultivation, known for producing the finest quality.

At Omar Ben Achour Company, we are passionate about providing premium, locally-sourced produce that meets global standards. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and innovation ensures that our customers experience only the best of Algeria's agricultural treasures. With a deep connection to the land and a forward-thinking approach, we aim to expand our footprint internationally, offering our signature products to markets around the world.
